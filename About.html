Legue of Legends Website

The beginner player does not have the expertise as a seasoned veteran of League of Legends (LoL), which makes it difficult to understand the various items, heroes, and movements in order for victory. Our website will provide players with gameplay analytics that will provide beginners and experts with valuable information on LoL. The aim of the project is to make the website a user-friendly environment for gamers to learn more about competitive gameplay strategies and techniques for LoL.
